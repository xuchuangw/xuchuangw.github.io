Xuchuang Wang

xuchuangwang@umass.edu

I am a postdoc at University of Massachusetts - Amherst, working with Don Towsley and Mohammad Hajiesmaili. I received my Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong under the guidance of John C.S. Lui.

I am on the 2025-2026 job market looking for full-time academia positions. Please feel free to reach out!

research highlights

  • multi-agent sequential decision-making:
    • communication-efficient algorithms (SIGMETRICS’25, NeurIPS’25, ICLR’23, AISTATS’23),
    • heterogenous cooperation algorithms (AAAI’25a, UAI’25, UAI’23),
    • shareable actions (ICML’22, IJCAI’22).
  • quantum network performance evaluation:
    • quantum network routing optimization (INFOCOM’25, INFOCOM’24),
    • quantum best arm identification (AAAI’25b).
    • quantum network tomography (In submission)
  • online learning theory:
    • bandit algorithms robust to adversarial attack (ICLR’25, L4DC’25),
    • fusing absolute and relative feedback (ICML’25),
    • multi-fidelity best arm identification (NeurIPS’23).
