Xuchuang Wang

Email: xuchuangwang@umass.edu

I am a postdoc at University of Massachusetts - Amherst, working with Don Towsley and Mohammad Hajiesmaili. I received my Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong under the guidance of John C.S. Lui.

My research is dedicated to the following three directions:

  • Multi-agent sequential decision-making
  • Quantum network performance evaluation
  • Online learning theory
