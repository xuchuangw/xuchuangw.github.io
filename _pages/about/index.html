I am a postdoc at University of Massachusetts Amherst, working with Don Towsley at ACQuIRe Lab and Mohammad Hajiesmaili at SOLAR Lab. I received my Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong under the guidance of John C.S. Lui at ANSR Lab. I also interned at Theory Center, Microsoft Research Asia, mentored by Wei Chen. I obtained my B.Eng. with Hons. from Xi’an Jiaotong University.

My current research focuses on sequential decision-making under uncertainty, aiming both to deepen theoretical understanding of decision-making with realistic feedback and to enhance practical performance in advanced application domains—especially multi-agent systems and quantum networks.

I am on the 2025-2026 job market looking for full-time academia positions. Please feel free to reach out!
